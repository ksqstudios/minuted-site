Frequently asked questions

Does Minuted work offline?

Yes, completely. Minuted doesn't make any network requests and works without internet.

Where is my data stored?

All data — your roster of people, meeting history, settings — stays on your device. It is never uploaded to a server or synced to the cloud. See the privacy policy for more.

Why do I need an hourly rate for someone on salary?

Minuted estimates hourly rate from annual salary by dividing by 2,080 (a standard working-hours-per-year figure). You can adjust this ratio in Settings.

The cost in the Live Activity isn't ticking — is that a bug?

No, this is intentional. iOS doesn't allow Live Activities to update custom numbers continuously. Minuted shows the elapsed time ticking and the burn rate as a static reference. Open the app to see the exact live cost.

How do I reset all my data?

Open Minuted, go to Settings (gear icon on the Roster tab), and tap "Reset all data." You'll be asked to confirm. This can't be undone.

Can I back up my data?

Not directly within Minuted. However, if you have iCloud backups enabled for your iPhone, your Minuted data is included in that backup per your iCloud settings.

Will there be an iPad or Apple Watch version?

Yes to both — iPad and Apple Watch support are planned for a future update. No firm timeline yet, but both are actively on the roadmap.

I have a feature request or found a bug. What should I do?

Email minuted@ksqstudios.com. Bug reports are most useful when they include the iPhone model, iOS version, and a description of what you expected versus what happened.
